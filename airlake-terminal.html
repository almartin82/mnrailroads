Airlake Terminal Railway

Lawrence A. Martin

 Fri 10 April 2015
Category: posts
Tags: short_line lakeville MN

The Airlake Terminal Railway, affiliated with Progressive Rail, is a short line railroad operating approximately 2.35 miles of track in a large industrial park in Lakeville, Minnesota. The Airlake Terminal RailRoad owns ten three-bay covered hoppers now in salt service and eight four-bay covered hoppers currently in the recycled battery case hauling service.
